FAQ for THE LEGENDZ Community

How Do I Get Into #general

We do not have an instant invite for the server. Instead, use this link: https://replit.com/discord

My repl won't start

If your repl is consistently failing to connect, open the Shell tab and type kill 1. Refresh your page and keep trying - it will probably work eventually. If you're getting a message like package operation failed but you know your packages are correct, try removing all your packages then typing kill 1 in shell and refreshing your page.

My repl keeps restarting

Even if you are using Always On or a webserver, your repl will restart every few hours. There is nothing you can do about this.

Hiding passwords, tokens and other secrets

You can hide your passwords, tokens, and other things you don't want people to see, using environment variables. Read this page to learn how to do this.

Changing NodeJS version

The Replit team are working on changing the NodeJS repl to use version 14. For now, you can use a Bash repl, as these have version 14 preinstalled. In a Bash repl, make a JavaScript file, for example index.js. In the file main.sh, type node index.js, replacing index.js with the name of the file you created.

Keeping repls online

There are two options for this. Either buy Hacker plan and use Always On, or create a webserver and use an HTTP uptime service. Here are tutorials for doing this for a Discord bot, in Python and JavaScript, but the concept works for anything in any language.

Server roles

Here are the main server roles, and what they represent:

  • @Replit Team: Reserved role. Held by Replit employees and interns.
  • @Head Mod: Reserved role. Held by the overseer of moderators. Acts as head of all server moderators (chat and event), manages server-wide rules, and resolves conflicts between mods. Has executive power over all server decisions and holds moderators accountable.
  • @Chat Mod: Appointed role. Group of moderators who supervise the behavior of server members in both text chat and voice channels, and enforces the server rules accordingly. Handles disciplinary decisions, mute and ban appeals, and manages conflict-resolution between server members.
  • @Event Mod: Appointed role. Group of moderators who organize, manage, and handle server-wide events, and moderate participants and their behavior during said events.
  • @Trial Mod: Appointed role. Mods in training - treat them with the same level of respect as full moderators.
  • @birthdayfellow: People whose birthday it is. Ping Zwack or Nayoar if it's your birthday!
  • @Replit Site Moderator: People who moderate the Replit website. Do not ping them for issues in this Discord server, and if you've found a bad post on Repl Talk, use the built-in report feature.
  • @Replit Hero: People who have made direct contributions to Replit. Do not ask for this role or do things specifically to try and get it.
  • @Royal Jammer: Anyone who has won a Replit codejam.
  • @Loyal Repler: Certain people who have been part of this community for a long time and are known and trusted. Do not ask for this role, or for someone else to have it.
